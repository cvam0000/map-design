Duration of Shutdown 5

93 of the 385 Internet shutdowns between 2012 and January 4th 2020 lasted less than 24 hours, 74 lasted between 24 and 72 hours, 43 lasted for over 72 hours, while no information was available on the respective duration of the remaining 209 Internet shutdowns. The non-availability of information is attributable chiefly to the fact that no public notifications are issued by the Government or Internet Service Providers before, during, or after shutdowns, leaving stakeholders outside the affected areas to source this information from available news reports, which do not consistently mention the duration’s for the shutdown.
